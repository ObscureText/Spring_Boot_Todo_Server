Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 30, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Todo App collects, stores, and handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email: Required for authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Name: Optional, stored in Firebase if provided.
  • Tasks: All tasks you create are stored in our database. Each user's data is private and cannot be accessed by others.

2. How We Handle Your Data

  • Emails and names are handled securely by Firebase and are encrypted in transit.
  • Tasks and other app data are stored without encryption, but access is restricted only to your account.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Account Deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time from the app.
  • Deleting your account removes all your personal information and tasks permanently from our servers.

4. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal data. While Firebase encrypts authentication data in transit, we cannot guarantee absolute security for stored tasks.
