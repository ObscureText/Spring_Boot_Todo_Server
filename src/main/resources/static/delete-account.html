How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to permanently delete your account and all your data from the Todo app:

  1. Open your profile: Tap the profile icon at the top-right corner of the app.
  2. Access account settings: You will see options like Logout and Delete Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account button: This is located at the bottom of the profile screen.
  4. Confirm your identity: Enter your password again for verification. This ensures that only you can delete your account.
  5. Account Deletion: Once confirmed, all your tasks, categories, and personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  6. Completion: You will receive a confirmation message that your account has been deleted.
